Mississippi Roast

Descriptions

Slow cooker roast recipe, also called Pepperoncini Roast. Serve with egg noodles or mashed.

Ingredients

  • 1 (4 pound) beef chuck roast
  • Â¼ cup butter

Steps

  1. Step 1 Place roast in a slow cooker. Form a pocket in the top of roast and place butter, pepperoncini peppers, ranch dressing mix, and au jus mix in the pocket.
  2. Step 2 Cook on Low for 8 hours.
